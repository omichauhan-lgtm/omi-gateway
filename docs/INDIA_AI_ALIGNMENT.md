# OMI: IndiaAI Strategic Alignment

## Core Doctrine
OMI operates under the principle that **Reliability always dominates economics**, aligning seamlessly with IndiaAI's mandate to establish trustworthy, sovereign, and scalable public AI infrastructure.

## Sovereign Data Residency
OMI ensures native integration with regional models (e.g., Sarvam AI, local vLLM deployments) to maintain strict data residency laws. The sovereign router natively detects Indic language intents and automatically channels them to local infrastructure.

## Public Sector Value
- **Language Equity:** Native support for Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and other languages without passing through English intermediaries.
- **Cost Frugality:** Intelligent routing avoids premium global models when a localized lightweight model demonstrates sufficient capability.
- **Accountability:** Full probabilistic governance and mathematical lineage for every AI interaction.
