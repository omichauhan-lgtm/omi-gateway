## Execution Tracks

### Track H1: Pilot Acquisition
*Objective: Secure first production users.*
- **Priority Targets:** Startup founders (agents, customer support, copilots), Universities (AI labs, research groups), and Public sector (state governance and innovation teams).
- **Required Assets:** Pilot Program Page, Onboarding Flow, and Pilot Success Metrics.

### Track H2: Public Proof Engine
*Objective: Continuously generate externally verifiable evidence.*
- **Mandatory Publications:** Monthly Reliability Report, Calibration Report, Cost Savings Report, and Sovereign AI Report.
- **Evidence Requirements:** Confidence intervals, statistical significance, and reproducibility.

### Track H3: Revenue Discovery
*Objective: Determine who pays and why.*
- **Experiments:** Free Open Source (Tier 1), Managed Cloud (Tier 2), Enterprise Support (Tier 3), and Reliability Dashboard (Tier 4).
- **Metrics:** Willingness to pay, support requests, and deployment interest.

### Track H4: IndiaAI Funding Preparation
*Objective: Become grant-ready.*
- **Mandatory Artifacts:** Executive Summary, Government Pitch Deck, Architecture Whitepaper, Sovereign Alignment Report, Benchmark Results, and Economics Report (compiled as Markdown or PDFs).
- **Messaging Focus:** Reliability, auditability, multilingual support, public infrastructure, and sovereignty.

### Track H5: Enterprise Conversion
*Objective: Prepare for enterprise pilots.*
- **Required Features:** Deployment templates, Observability dashboard, SLA metrics, Audit logs, and Governance reports.
- **Success Definition:** Secure first enterprise pilot.

### Track H6: Ecosystem Growth
*Objective: Grow beyond the founding team.*
- **Required Actions:** Public Roadmap, Good First Issues, Contributor Rewards, and Monthly Community Updates.
- **Metrics:** 10 Contributors, 100 Stars, 25 Forks.

### Track H7: Real-World Telemetry Moat
*Objective: Accumulate proprietary operational intelligence.*
- **Moat Assets:** Provider drift timelines, calibration history, multilingual reliability, escalation patterns, hallucination topology, and sovereign model performance.
- *Strategic Rule:* The long-term moat is not routing. The moat is accumulated reliability intelligence.

## Anti-Overengineering Hard Rules

1. **Rule 1:** No new governance engine without user demand.
2. **Rule 2:** No new telemetry system without adoption impact.
3. **Rule 3:** No new architecture phase without external validation.
4. **Rule 4:** No complexity that does not improve trust or adoption.

> [!TIP]
> **Review Question:** Will this work help acquire users, secure funding, improve trust, or strengthen the data moat? If not, deprioritize it.

## Execution Protocol

- **Daily:** Review adoption metrics, production telemetry, user feedback, and reliability changes.
- **Weekly:** Publish ecosystem updates, review pilot opportunities, review contributor growth, and review grant opportunities.
- **Monthly:** Publish public reports, update roadmap, review revenue experiments, and review institutional outreach.
