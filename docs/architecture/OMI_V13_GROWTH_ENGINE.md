# OMI V13 Growth Engine Constitution

## Meta
- **Classification:** Sovereign Cognitive Reliability Infrastructure
- **Maturity Stage:** Adoption, Distribution, & External Validation
- **Mission:** Establish OMI as the trusted, adopted reliability and calibration layer for sovereign and enterprise AI platforms.
- **North Star:** Secure the first 10 signed pilots, reach 100 active users, and publish verifiable public-facing trust assets.

---

## Strategic Truth

```yaml
principle: >
  Technical superiority without adoption is zero-value infrastructure.
  Every engineering decision must now optimize for adoption, distribution,
  transparency, and institutional trust.
```

---

## Primary Objectives

1. **User & Pilot Acquisition:** Shift focus from internal validation algorithms to customer-facing validation pipelines.
2. **Dynamic Evidence Sharing:** Enable enterprises, contributors, and governments to verify OMI's metrics dynamically.
3. **Funding & Grant Readiness:** Align OMI's measurable outcomes directly with the requirements of sovereign grants (e.g. IndiaAI, MeitY) and venture platforms.

---

## Core Pillars

### 1. User Acquisition & Pilot Program
- Build open pipelines allowing developers to apply for private endpoints.
- Serve dynamic dashboards tracking accepted vs pending pilots, aggregated request volumes, and proven reliability gains across industrial verticals.

### 2. Weighted Sovereign Score Model
- Avoid opaque, hard-coded alignment scores.
- Evaluate providers using a composite model of 6 weighted components:
  - **India-Hosted Inference (25%):** Ensures local latency and legal compliance.
  - **Indic Language Performance (25%):** Assesses multi-dialect alignment accuracy.
  - **Data Residency Compliance (20%):** Validates localized data plane isolation.
  - **Tokenizer Efficiency for Indic (10%):** Minimizes overhead and context usage.
  - **Latency for Indic Queries (10%):** Ensures sub-threshold response speed.
  - **Auditability and Transparency (10%):** Guarantees forensic tracking capabilities.

### 3. Hybrid Case Studies
- Combine fixed architectural snapshots and qualitative lessons learned with live-updating database telemetry metrics (total routed requests, cost saved, escalation rates, and accuracy indices).
- Provide stable reference cases with real-time empirical verification.

### 4. Continuous Funding Readiness Audit
- Measure OMI against 6 key institutional axes:
  - Adoption Score
  - Reliability Score
  - Sovereign Score
  - Benchmark Score
  - Evidence Score
  - Pilot Score
- Maintain a dynamic Readiness Indicator directly guiding monthly releases.
