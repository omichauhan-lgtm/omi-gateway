## Strategic Tracks

### Track G1: Real-World Validation
*Objective: Gather real-world telemetry.*
- **Mandatory Requirements:** Public Sandbox, Usage Tracking, Telemetry Retention, and Reliability Measurement.
- **Success Metrics:** Minimum 10,000 requests, 100 users, and 30 telemetry days.

### Track G2: Public Evidence Expansion
*Objective: Make reliability visible.*
- **Mandatory Publications:** Monthly Reliability Report, Monthly Calibration Report, Monthly Provider Drift Report, and Quarterly Sovereign AI Report.
- **Required Visualizations:** Calibration Curves, Confidence Intervals, Provider Rankings, Drift Timelines, and Cost Savings metrics.

### Track G3: Enterprise Readiness
*Objective: Become pilot-ready.*
- **Mandatory Capabilities:** Completed Audit Logs & RBAC, Deployment Guides, Docker Images, Observability Dashboard, and OpenAPI Documentation.
- **Required Metrics:** Measurable Cost Savings, Reliability Gain, and Calibration Improvement.

### Track G4: IndiaAI & Government Alignment
*Objective: Position OMI as Public AI Infrastructure.*
- **Messaging Doctrine:**
  - *Avoid:* "AGI", "superintelligence", "AI wrapper", "chatbot platform".
  - *Emphasize:* "AI Reliability Infrastructure", "Sovereign AI Governance", "Multilingual Calibration", "Public Digital Infrastructure", "Auditability", "Trust".
- **Required Deliverables:** Executive Summary, Government Pitch Deck, IndiaAI Alignment Report, Sovereign Benchmark Report, and Public Use Case Catalog.
- **Target Use Cases:** Citizen assistance, Grievance redressal, Administrative copilots, Education support, and Multilingual services.

### Track G5: Open-Source Expansion
*Objective: Create ecosystem gravity.*
- **Required Assets:** Maintained Contributor Guide (CONTRIBUTING.md), Issue Templates, Public Roadmap, Public Benchmark Suite, and enabled GitHub Discussions.
- **Growth Targets:** 100 Stars (Goal 1), 1,000 Stars (Goal 2); 10 Contributors (Goal 1), 50 Contributors (Goal 2).

### Track G6: Data Moat Development
*Objective: Build non-replicable operational intelligence.*
- **Strategic Assets:** Provider drift history, Calibration timelines, Hallucination patterns, Escalation economics, Indic reliability metrics, and Multilingual failure modes.
- *Principle:* Models are replaceable. Reliability telemetry is not.

## Anti-Overengineering Constraints

1. **Rule 1:** No new governance engine without external demand.
2. **Rule 2:** No new telemetry layer without measurable value.
3. **Rule 3:** No architectural expansion before adoption analysis.
4. **Rule 4:** No complexity that does not improve reliability, trust, or adoption.

> [!TIP]
> **Review Question:** Will this feature help us acquire users, improve reliability, generate evidence, secure funding, or strengthen the moat? If not, deprioritize it.

## Execution Protocol

- **Daily:** Review production metrics, public dashboard, user feedback, and reliability changes.
- **Weekly:** Publish metrics summary, update benchmarks, review adoption progress, and review grant opportunities.
- **Monthly:** Publish reliability report, calibration report, cost savings report, and update public roadmap.

OMI wins not by becoming the most complex AI infrastructure system. OMI wins by becoming the most trusted, measurable, auditable, and evidence-backed reliability layer in the sovereign AI ecosystem.
